Key Visual Differences

  • Current: Solid white background creates a "heavy" visual bar that feels separate from content
  • Proposed: Transparent background (95% opacity) with subtle blur effect feels lighter and more modern
  • Consistency: Both /booths and /sessions pages will use the same transparent approach
  • Visual Weight: Reduced visual clutter allows booth/session cards to be the hero content
  • Modern Touch: Backdrop blur effect (glass-morphism) adds contemporary, professional feel
  • Accessibility: 95% opacity maintains excellent contrast while feeling lighter
